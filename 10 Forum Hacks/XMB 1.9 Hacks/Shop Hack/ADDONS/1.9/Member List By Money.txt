Description: This addon will add the ability to sort the member list by money.

Compatibility: XMB v1.9

Code Designed By: nfpunk/Flixon

* edit misc.php

FIND
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    $order = "uid"; 
}
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    $order = "uid";
}
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
